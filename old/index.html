What is Jimbo?

Jimbo is an app that Rami Kabar and Stephen Oliver have been working on for the past year that allows you to easily buy and sell items with your friends! The purpose of the app is to allow you to spend more time selling than trying to sell.

The app is currently in development and will be available for beta testing really soon! To be one of the first to try the app, subscribe above using your email and we'll keep you updated with what's happening!
